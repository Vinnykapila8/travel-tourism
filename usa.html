New York City

New York City is known for Times Square, Central Park, and the Statue of Liberty, making it a global cultural hub.

Washington D.C.

Washington D.C. is the capital of the United States, home to the White House, Capitol, and national monuments.

Los Angeles

Los Angeles is famous for Hollywood, sunny beaches, and its vibrant entertainment industry.

Chicago

Chicago is known for its impressive skyline, deep-dish pizza, and cultural landmarks like Millennium Park.

San Francisco

San Francisco is iconic for the Golden Gate Bridge, cable cars, and its diverse neighborhoods.

Las Vegas

Las Vegas is renowned for its casinos, nightlife, and dazzling entertainment shows in the Nevada desert.

Miami

Miami is famous for its beaches, Cuban culture, and vibrant nightlife along South Beach.

Honolulu

Honolulu, on the island of Oahu, is known for Waikiki Beach, volcanic landscapes, and Hawaiian culture.
