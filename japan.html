JAPAN

Tokyo

Tokyo is Japan’s bustling capital, known for its skyscrapers, technology, and traditional temples like Senso-ji.

Kyoto

Kyoto is famous for its historic shrines, geisha districts, and stunning cherry blossoms in spring.

Osaka

Osaka is known for its vibrant street food, Osaka Castle, and lively entertainment districts.

Nara

Nara is home to friendly free-roaming deer and the Great Buddha statue at Todai-ji Temple.

Hiroshima

Hiroshima is remembered for the Peace Memorial Park and its message of resilience and hope.

Sapporo

Sapporo is famous for its snow festival, ski resorts, and delicious miso ramen.

Fukuoka

Fukuoka is a coastal city known for its ancient temples, shopping, and Hakata ramen.

Mount Fuji

Mount Fuji is Japan’s iconic volcano, a UNESCO site, and a popular destination for climbers and photographers.
