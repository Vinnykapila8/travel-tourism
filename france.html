FRANCE

Paris

Paris is the capital of France, known for the Eiffel Tower, Louvre Museum, and its romantic atmosphere.

Nice

Nice is a coastal city on the French Riviera, famous for its beaches, Promenade des Anglais, and Mediterranean charm.

Lyon

Lyon is renowned for its gastronomy, Renaissance architecture, and role as a UNESCO World Heritage site.

Marseille

Marseille is France’s oldest city, known for its port, multicultural vibe, and landmarks like Notre-Dame de la Garde.

Bordeaux

Bordeaux is world-famous for its wine, elegant architecture, and historic city center.

Strasbourg

Strasbourg is known for its medieval old town, Gothic cathedral, and as the seat of the European Parliament.

Mont Saint-Michel

Mont Saint-Michel is a stunning island commune with a medieval abbey rising dramatically from the sea.

Lille

Lille is a vibrant northern city, known for its Flemish architecture, lively markets, and cultural festivals.
