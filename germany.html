Berlin

Berlin is Germany’s capital, known for its history and vibrant culture.

Hamburg

Hamburg is a major port city famous for its harbor and canals.

Heidelberg is renowned for its old university and romantic castle.

Nuremberg

Nuremberg is known for medieval architecture and Christmas markets.

Munich

Munich is famous for Oktoberfest and Bavarian traditions.

Cologne

Cologne is home to the stunning Cologne Cathedral and rich history.

Dresden

Dresden is known for its baroque architecture and art treasures.

Stuttgart

Stuttgart is an automotive hub, home to Mercedes-Benz and Porsche.
