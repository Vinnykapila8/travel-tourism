GREECE

Athens

Athens is the capital of Greece, famous for the Acropolis, Parthenon, and its rich ancient history.

Santorini

Santorini is known for its whitewashed houses, blue-domed churches, and stunning sunsets over the Aegean Sea.

Mykonos

Mykonos is a lively island famous for its beaches, nightlife, and charming Cycladic architecture.

Delphi

Delphi was once considered the center of the world in ancient Greece, home to the Oracle and sacred temples.

Crete

Crete is Greece’s largest island, known for its beaches, mountains, and the ancient Minoan palace of Knossos.

Rhodes

Rhodes is famous for its medieval Old Town, ancient ruins, and the site of the Colossus of Rhodes.

Thessaloniki

Thessaloniki is a vibrant city with Byzantine monuments, lively markets, and a rich cultural scene.

Meteora

Meteora is renowned for its monasteries perched atop towering rock formations, offering breathtaking views.
