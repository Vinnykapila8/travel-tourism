MEXICO

Chichen Itza

Chichen Itza is a UNESCO World Heritage site and one of the New Seven Wonders of the World, showcasing Mayan architecture.

Mexico City

Mexico City is the vibrant capital, known for its historic center, museums, and the iconic Zócalo square.

Cancún

Cancún is famous for its turquoise beaches, resorts, and lively nightlife along the Caribbean coast.

Oaxaca

Oaxaca is celebrated for its indigenous culture, colorful festivals, and traditional cuisine like mole.

Guadalajara

Guadalajara is the birthplace of mariachi music and tequila, with a rich colonial heritage.

Tulum

Tulum is known for its cliffside Mayan ruins overlooking the Caribbean Sea and eco-friendly resorts.

Monterrey

Monterrey is a modern industrial hub surrounded by dramatic mountains and natural parks.

Puebla

Puebla is famous for its colonial architecture, Talavera pottery, and the culinary dish mole poblano.
