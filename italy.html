ITALY

Rome

Rome is Italy’s capital, known for the Colosseum, Roman Forum, and Vatican City with St. Peter’s Basilica.

Venice

Venice is famous for its canals, gondolas, and landmarks like St. Mark’s Square and the Rialto Bridge.

Florence

Florence is the cradle of the Renaissance, home to masterpieces by Michelangelo and the iconic Duomo.

Milan

Milan is Italy’s fashion capital, known for the Gothic cathedral, La Scala opera house, and modern design.

Naples

Naples is renowned for its historic center, proximity to Pompeii, and as the birthplace of pizza.

Pisa

Pisa is world-famous for its Leaning Tower, part of the Piazza dei Miracoli UNESCO site.

Cinque Terre

Cinque Terre is a coastal region with colorful cliffside villages, scenic hiking trails, and stunning sea views.

Sicily

Sicily is Italy’s largest island, known for Mount Etna, ancient ruins, and a rich blend of cultures.
