Madrid

Madrid is Spain’s capital, known for the Royal Palace, Prado Museum, and lively plazas like Puerta del Sol.

Barcelona

Barcelona is famous for Gaudí’s architecture, including the Sagrada Família, and its Mediterranean beaches.

Seville

Seville is known for flamenco dancing, the Alcázar palace, and its stunning Gothic cathedral.

Granada

Granada is home to the Alhambra, a Moorish palace and fortress with breathtaking gardens and views.

Valencia

Valencia is famous for its futuristic City of Arts and Sciences, beaches, and the traditional dish paella.

Bilbao

Bilbao is known for the Guggenheim Museum, modern architecture, and its Basque cultural heritage.

Toledo

Toledo is a historic city with medieval streets, blending Christian, Jewish, and Muslim influences.

Málaga

Málaga is a coastal city known for its beaches, Picasso Museum, and lively Andalusian culture.
